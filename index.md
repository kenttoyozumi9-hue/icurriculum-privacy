# Privacy Policy — ICUrriculum

**Last updated: March 2026**

ICUrriculum ("the App") is a course planning and campus life app for students at International Christian University (ICU). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you register, we collect:
- Your **name**
- Your **email address**
- Your **password** (stored as a secure hash — we never see your plain-text password)
- Your **major code** and **graduation year**

### Usage Data
- Courses you enroll in or add to your timetable
- Course ratings and votes you submit
- Friends you connect with within the app

### Automatically Collected
- Basic server logs (IP address, request timestamps) for security and debugging purposes

We do **not** collect location data, contacts, camera/microphone access, or any device identifiers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and operate the App (login, timetable, course tracking)
- To show your progress toward graduation requirements
- To display your timetable to friends you have connected with (only if you enable this)
- To allow companies to list internship opportunities visible to all users
- To improve the App based on anonymous usage patterns

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 3. Internship Listings & Commercial Use

Companies may pay to list internship opportunities within the App. These listings are visible to all users. We do **not** share your personal information (name, email, etc.) with these companies without your explicit consent.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:
- **Service providers** necessary to operate the App (e.g., cloud hosting on Render)
- **Legal requirements** — if required by law or to protect user safety

---

## 5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

---

## 6. Security

We use industry-standard measures including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Hashed passwords (bcrypt)
- Secure token-based authentication (JWT)

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for university students (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **kent@icu.ac.jp**

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If changes are significant, we will notify users within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
